About Rest & Restore Therapy

Warm, personalized therapy rooted in healing and connection.

Rest & Restore Therapy currently serves clients through telehealth and is expanding into a collaborative group practice. The practice is built on the values of reliability, authenticity, and compassion, with a goal of creating a safe place for healing, growth, and meaningful change.

Our approach

Sessions prioritize undivided attention, genuine care, and practical skills. The style is warm, light-hearted, and goal-oriented, integrating approaches such as CBT, ACT, motivational interviewing, mindfulness-based strategies, and somatic awareness.

Faith-integrated care

A faith-based Christian perspective is available for clients who request it, while therapy remains open and welcoming to people of all backgrounds.

Connect, Reflect, and Direct

Sessions often begin with checking in and helping you settle, then exploring what has been happening, and finally moving into psychoeducation, new coping strategies, or following up on skills from previous sessions.

Telehealth model

All services are currently offered through a HIPAA-compliant video platform. Services are not offered by telephone or in person at this time.
